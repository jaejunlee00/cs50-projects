Since 1993, Texas Roadhouse began to serve Hand-Cut Steaks, Fall-Off-The-Bone Ribs, Made-From-Scratch Sides, and Fresh-Baked Bread. They handcraft almost everything they serve and provide larger portions so that you get more food for your dollar. I can definitely say this franchise is the most famous steakhouse in Austin, Texas. Their steak is indeed great, but their complimentary breads are also fantastic. The breads are coated with butter so the outside is crunch and inside is soft. They are served with their special sweet honey butter which makes you can't stop eating more breads.
