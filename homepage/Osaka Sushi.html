Osaka Mansun Sushi is the very first sushi restaurant in Austin, Texas and number one in Yelp review for sushi restuarants in Austin. They carry prime sushi with extraordinary quality and freshness. This place is my most favorite sushi restaurant and I visit at least once in two weeks. Not only the dishes are great but also chefs and staffs are very nice and professional. My favorite is Mansun special combo, where Mansun means "full."
