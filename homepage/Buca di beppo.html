Buca di beppo serves an authentic family style Italian Food. The portion can be either small (3 person portion) or large (5 person portion). Their food quality is prime and staffs are nice. What's interesting about this restaurant is the food's portion. If you order any menu as large, it comes inside a GIANT bowl with tons of food. Food taste is just as good as its portion. This Italian restaurant is the best option to visit with family or friend.
