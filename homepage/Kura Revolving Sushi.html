Do you want to experience a new, dynamic, and entertaining sushi dine-in experience? Then you should visit Kura Revolving Sushi. It's the very first revolving sushi restuarant in Austin, Texas. There is a long rail with a variety of sushi plates. If you see your favorites you can simply pick up those dishes and enjoy. At the end, there's a slot you can insert your empty dishes. If you put more than 15 dishes you can get a surprising prize, too.
